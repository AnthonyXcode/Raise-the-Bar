What is Wealth Cockpit?

One platform for banks and EAMs to streamline advisory and improve client engagement.

Wealth Cockpit is our flagship wealth management proposition designed to provide a single, integrated platform for banks and external asset managers (EAMs) to streamline advisory workflows, gain deeper portfolio insights, and improve client engagement.

Instead of switching between multiple systems to review portfolios, log interactions, or generate reports, users can access everything through one unified interface.

Wealth Cockpit is available in two tailored offerings:

Wealth Cockpit For Banks

Designed for private banks and wealth managers, the platform provides dedicated views for both relationship managers (RMs) and end clients.

Wealth Cockpit for EAMs

Designed for EAMs who manage client portfolios across multiple custodians, consolidating data, monitoring performance, and streamlining reporting across different banking relationships.
